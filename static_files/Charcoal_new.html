Charcoal.

The charcoal drawings started the very first atempts Arthurx ever made to make a drawing. Actually his girlfriend came home from a course drawing for beginners with as homework to make a few charcoals.

As paper the wood holding newspaper paper was used. This is blank when drawn on but shades to a rouge creme color when hung at a wall. All kind of drawings were made then, realistic and more abstract. The reason to go on was that each work told a different story.
