Dresden

The last day of the "KunstlerMeile" in Dresden.
Art sleeping, tired after 3 days live painting.

We were innvited by Andrea Ritz for the "KunstlerMeile" of the Stadfest 650 Jahr Dresden.
We had met her in Berlin, where she rented a room in the same house where we were staying.
She offered us her apartment and took self place in the apartment of her father downstairs.
It was very close to the Elbe and the Bruhlse Terasse.

Arthur, Sebastian, son of Andrea and Andrea
Arrival at the Bruhlsche terasse.

In front of the "Kunsthochschule" we took our booth.



The first painting on Friday afternoon,
The "Frauenkirche", build up after WWO II, beating heart of the touristic center.
It is a bit sad that all those rebuild buildings are surrounded by a big ring of intense traffic, then a ring of phantasyless, new, sterilized buildings and then finally some plots of vivid and beautiful town is found in Aussere Neustadt and on the way to Blaues Wunder.
